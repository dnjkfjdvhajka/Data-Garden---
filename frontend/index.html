My Data Garden

《诗经》草木有灵，芳华寄情，四时有序，静美无言。
Plants hold spirit, flowers bear emotion; all follows nature's rhythm, serene and silent.

Welcome to my visual interactive project! It's all about turning abstract ideas, cultural vibes and complex info into something you can see, touch and fall in love with. Blending elegant, detailed visuals (inspired by traditional Chinese aesthetics) with smooth, simple interactions, this interface is made to feel intuitive and immersive—no fancy skills needed, just pure enjoyment as you explore. Perfect for UI display, it's designed to catch your eye and keep you engaged, one easy, beautiful interaction at a time.
